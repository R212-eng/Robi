ABOUT

Safe life is a non-profit organization aimed at helping the young generation
to overcome their challenges and meet their life goals.
It was founded in 2011 by a group of university students who were curoius
about the situation in their surrounding.
Its scope extends to protecting youth from all kinds of addiction,
mainly focusing on substance abuse, through providing awareness
on the adverse effects of addiction and guiding them on their way out of it.
